Can you explain relative velocity with an example?

Sure! Relative velocity is how fast one object moves compared to another.

Example: If you're on a train moving at 60 km/h and walk forward at 5 km/h, someone on the ground sees you moving at 65 km/h total.

Formula: vrelative = vobject - vreference
