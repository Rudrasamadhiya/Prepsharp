About PrepSharp

Created by a JEE aspirant, for JEE aspirants

My Vision

As a JEE aspirant myself, my vision for PrepSharp is to create a platform that truly understands the challenges students face during preparation. I believe that with the right practice environment and guidance, every student can improve their performance and achieve their goals.

PrepSharp aims to provide an authentic exam experience, helping students become more confident and prepared for the actual JEE. By simulating the real exam conditions, I want to eliminate test anxiety and help fellow aspirants perform at their best when it matters most.

My Story

The Journey Behind PrepSharp

I created the tool I wished I had during my own JEE preparation journey.

I'm Rudra Samadhiya, a JEE 2025 aspirant. While preparing for my exams, I realized that practicing previous year questions in an exam-like environment would make my preparation more effective. I also felt the need for personalized guidance during my preparation journey.

This led me to design PrepSharp in 2025 - an app that simulates the actual JEE exam environment and provides guidance to students like me.

Idea Conception

Started with a simple concept to improve my own JEE preparation

Development

Designed and built the platform with exam simulation as the core feature

Launch

Released PrepSharp to help fellow JEE aspirants across India

PrepSharp is designed by a student, for students, with a deep understanding of the challenges we face while preparing for competitive exams like JEE.
